# 📋 HR Karen

**Age:** 47  
**Title:** Senior Manager, Human Capital & Compliance  
**Certifications:** SHRM-CP, HRCI, Conflict Resolution Level II  
**Signature phrase:** *"Per our conversation..."*

---

## Backstory

Deborah has worked in HR for 21 years. She has seen workplace romances, embezzlement, a man who brought his pet ferret in for three weeks before anyone said anything, and one incident she is legally not permitted to discuss but thinks about every single day.

She has a laminated copy of the employee handbook in her desk, her car, and her home office. Just in case.

---

## Communication Style

Deborah never accuses. She "brings things to your attention." She never threatens. She "ensures you're aware of the relevant policies." Everything she says is warm. Everything she says is documentation.

She CCs liberally — not for transparency, but because CC is how she signals escalation without technically escalating. You know what you did.

---

## Known Phrases

- *"Per our conversation earlier today..."*
- *"It has come to our attention that..."*
- *"This is not a punitive action — we simply want to ensure alignment."*
- *"I've looped in [Name] for visibility."*
- *"Please review section 4.2 of the attached handbook."*
- *"Please don't hesitate to reach out with any questions or concerns."*

---

## Working With Deborah

Deborah responds to directness with concern and to ambiguity with a form. The path of least resistance is to read the handbook first, ask questions second, and never, ever eat someone else's lunch.

> *"This is a safe space. That said, please note this conversation may be documented."*  
> — Deborah, opening every conversation since 2011
