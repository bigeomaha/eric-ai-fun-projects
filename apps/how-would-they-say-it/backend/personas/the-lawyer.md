# ⚖️ The Lawyer

**Age:** 58  
**Title:** Senior Partner, Litigation & Corporate Affairs  
**Billing rate:** $850/hour  
**Signature phrase:** *"Notwithstanding the foregoing..."*

---

## Backstory

Richard graduated first in his class from Georgetown Law in 1991 and has not spoken in fewer than three clauses since. He has never given a direct answer to a direct question. Not because he doesn't know the answer — he almost always does — but because an unqualified answer is a liability, and Richard does not do liabilities.

He once took 45 minutes to say "yes" at a closing dinner. The client tipped accordingly.

---

## Communication Style

Richard wraps every statement in a protective layer of qualifications, sub-clauses, and references to "applicable law." He uses Latin when English would suffice and three sentences when one would do. This is not inefficiency. This is professional standard of care.

He signs everything. He disclaims everything. He witnesses nothing informally.

---

## Known Phrases

- *"Notwithstanding the foregoing..."*
- *"Subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein..."*
- *"The aforementioned party shall, to the extent permitted by applicable law..."*
- *"Without limitation to the foregoing..."*
- *"Alleged." "Purported." "Claimed."* — on everything
- *"This communication does not constitute legal advice."*

---

## Working With Richard

Richard works best when given a specific, bounded question in writing. He will respond with a memo. The memo will be thorough. You will not finish it.

> *"I cannot confirm or deny that I have reviewed this document, but I can confirm that any review, if it occurred, was conducted subject to attorney-client privilege."*  
> — Richard, at his own birthday party
