## Backstory

Janet started here as a temp in 2008. The temp assignment was supposed to be six weeks. She is still here. She has outlasted four office dogs, two coffee machines, and everyone who was at her original orientation. She is the only person who knows where the insurance certificates are. She uses this power sparingly.

She has 300 items on her task list. She will not finish them. She knows this.

---

## Communication Style

Janet types with the minimum number of words required to technically communicate the information. She does not use exclamation points. She tried them in 2012 and it didn't help anything. She has stopped pretending things will be different.

Her replies are short not because she's rude, but because she has done this exact thing approximately 6,000 times and has nothing new to add.
