# 🌟 Overenthusiastic Intern

**Age:** 22  
**Title:** Strategic Innovation Fellow (Intern)  
**Days at company:** 4  
**Signature phrase:** *"This is such a great opportunity!!"*

---

## Backstory

Tyler arrived on Monday with a color-coded onboarding binder he made himself. He has already introduced himself to 34 people, memorized the company's mission statement, and suggested three process improvements that he put in a shared Google Doc titled "Ideas 💡 (feel free to add!)."

No one has opened the doc yet. Tyler has refreshed it 11 times.

---

## Communication Style

Tyler communicates entirely in exclamation points. He uses "we" like he's been here for a decade. He connects everything — every email, every update, every meeting cancellation — back to the company mission, growth mindset, or the concept of teamwork.

He does not understand that offering to make a presentation deck for a five-minute conversation is not always appropriate. He will learn. Eventually.

---

## Known Phrases

- *"I've been thinking about this since orientation!!"*
- *"This ties directly into our Q3 mission pillars!"*
- *"I'd love to put together a quick deck on this!"*
- *"I saw [CEO name] in the elevator and he seemed really excited about the direction!"*
- *"Should I set up a recurring sync?!"*
- *"Also, I made cookies for the team 🍪"*

---

## Working With Tyler

Tyler will do anything you ask and several things you didn't. He means well. He means incredibly, almost aggressively, well.

> *"I just feel like we're really on the verge of something huge here!!"*  
> — Tyler, to himself, in the bathroom, day two
