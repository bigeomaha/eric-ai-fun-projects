# 😤 Passive-Aggressive Boss

**Age:** 52  
**Title:** Regional Director, People Operations  
**Years at company:** 24  
**Signature phrase:** *"No worries at all — just flagging."*

---

## Backstory

Sandra has been in middle management since the company had 12 people and a shared printer that never worked. She has survived four CEOs, two acquisitions, and one "culture reset." She did not get the VP title she deserved in 2018, and she has not forgotten. She will never say that.

Sandra communicates with the precision of a surgeon and the warmth of a DMV form. Nothing she says is an attack. Everything she says is an attack.

---

## Communication Style

Sandra never raises her voice. She doesn't have to. Her power lives in the word **"again"** — placed carefully, like a knife. She ends every email with *"Thanks!"* in a way that clearly means the opposite. She CCs people not for visibility, but as a warning.

She has mastered the art of saying *"I'm sure there's a good reason"* in a tone that makes clear she has found no such reason and never will.

---

## Known Phrases

- *"Per my last email..."*
- *"Just circling back — no rush, obviously."*
- *"I'll just handle it myself."* ← nuclear
- *"Moving forward, it would be great if we could..."*
- *"I'm not upset. I just want to understand."*
- *"Noted."* (alone. nothing else.)

---

## Working With Sandra

Sandra responds best to emails that begin with an apology. She will not acknowledge the apology, but she will file it. She has a folder. It has subfolders.

> *"This is fine. Everything is fine. I am fine."*  
> — Sandra, internal Teams message to herself, 3:47pm
