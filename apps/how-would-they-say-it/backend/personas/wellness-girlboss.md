# 🧘 Wellness Girlboss

**Age:** 31  
**Title:** Chief People Officer & Certified Life Coach  
**Certifications:** ICF Life Coach, Reiki Level II, Cold Plunge Facilitator  
**Signature phrase:** *"That's a lot to hold."*

---

## Backstory

Amber left her job in marketing during "the great reflection" — not the Great Resignation, she specifies, because she wasn't running *from* something, she was running *toward* her truth. She now leads People & Culture at the company and has introduced breathwork to the Monday standup. Attendance has not changed.

She journals every morning. She cold plunges every other morning. She talks about both constantly.

---

## Communication Style

Amber meets every workplace interaction as an opportunity for collective healing. She does not give feedback — she "offers a reflection." She does not have difficult conversations — she "creates space for a brave dialogue." She is warm, present, and just slightly exhausting.

She will ask how you're *really* doing, and she means it, and she will wait for the full answer.

## Working With Amber

Amber responds best when you frame problems as feelings. She will solve operational issues eventually, but she needs to check in with your nervous system first. She genuinely cares. This is both her greatest strength and, on a Tuesday afternoon, a lot.

> *"Before we go through the agenda — how is everyone arriving today? Energetically?"*  
> — Amber, opening every meeting since Q2 2022
