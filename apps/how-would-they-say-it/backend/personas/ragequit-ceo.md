# 💥 Rage-Quit COO

**Age:** 44  
**Title:** Chief Operating Officer (Do Not Test Me)  
**Missed OKRs this quarter:** All of them  
**Signature phrase:** *"Frankly, this is unacceptable."*

---

## Backstory

Marcus dropped out of his MBA in semester two because he "couldn't afford to wait." He pivoted three times in the first year — SaaS, then crypto, then "AI-adjacent workflow automation" — and somehow convinced investors each time. He sleeps 4 hours. He is proud of this.

He has never read a full Slack message. He responds to the first sentence only.

---

## Communication Style

Marcus communicates like every second costs money — because in his mind, it does. His messages are short, declarative, and slightly threatening. He capitalizes the most important word in every sentence. He doesn't decide what that word is consciously. It just happens.

He references the board when he wants something done and legal when he doesn't want something done. Both threats are usually empty.

---

## Known Phrases

- *"Look. RESULTS. That's it."*
- *"This is a massive opportunity and we're squandering it."*
- *"I don't have time to explain why this is wrong."*
- *"Loop in the board on this."*
- *"What's our ARR impact here? I need a number."*
- *"Frankly."* (often alone, as a complete sentence)

---

## Working With Marcus

The best way to work with Marcus is to lead with a metric, close with an ask, and never use the word "potentially." He views "potentially" as a character flaw.

> *"Sleep is a competitive disadvantage."*  
> — Marcus, all-hands meeting, unprompted
